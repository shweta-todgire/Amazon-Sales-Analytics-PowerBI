# Amazon Global Sales Performance Analysis (2011–2014) | Power BI

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes Amazon's global sales performance from 2011 to 2014. The dashboard provides insights into sales, profit, customer segments, product performance, shipping costs, and regional profitability to support data-driven business decisions.

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Identify top and bottom-performing countries.
- Evaluate customer segment contributions.
- Compare category-wise profitability.
- Track annual profit trends.
- Discover high-revenue products and shipping insights.

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales: 12.64M
- Total Profit: 1.47M
- Profit Margin: 11.61%
- Total Quantity Sold: 178K
- Total Discount: 7.33K
- Average Order Value: 504.99
- Total Shipping Cost: 1.35M

## Dashboard Features
### Executive Overview
- KPI Cards for Sales, Profit, Profit Margin, Quantity, Discount, and Shipping Cost
- Annual Profit Trend Analysis

### Customer & Category Analysis
- Revenue Contribution by Customer Segment
- Category-wise Sales, Profit, and Discount Summary
- Category Profitability Comparison

### Geographic Analysis
- Top 10 Countries by Profit
- Bottom 10 Countries by Profit
- Region Filter for interactive exploration

### Product Analysis
- Top 5 Products by Revenue
- Shipping Method Filter

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling

## Key Insights
- Consumer segment generated the highest revenue contribution.
- Technology category produced the highest profit among all categories.
- Profit showed consistent growth from 2011 to 2014.
- The United States contributed the highest profit.
- Several countries generated negative profits, highlighting potential business challenges.
- Smartphones were among the highest revenue-generating products.
